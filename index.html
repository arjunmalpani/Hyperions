About Us

Hyperion serves as the backbone of our college's cultural ecosystem, organizing flagship events like Exploranza and Arohana. Beyond these grand celebrations, Hyperion plays a pivotal role in unearthing and nurturing student talent across diverse fields. The committee takes charge of streamlining the activities of various cultural societies, ensuring smooth operations and fostering a vibrant campus environment. Moreover, it actively collaborates with sponsors to host a plethora of engaging and interactive events, amplifying the college's cultural vibrance.

In addition to organizing in-house events, Hyperion encourages and supports the participation of its societies in inter-college competitions, representing the institution on larger platforms. At the start of every academic year, a committee comprising faculty and elected student representatives is formed to oversee and monitor the society's functioning. This democratic and collaborative approach ensures the societies run efficiently while fostering leadership and teamwork among students.
